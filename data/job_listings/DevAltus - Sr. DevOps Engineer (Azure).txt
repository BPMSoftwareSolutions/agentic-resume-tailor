Full job description
DevAltus is a cloud-native consultancy delivering secure, scalable, and high-performance infrastructure solutions for enterprise clients. We’re hiring a Senior Azure DevOps Engineer to lead the design, automation, and optimization of mission-critical systems in Microsoft Azure.
If you’re a proactive, senior-level cloud engineer who excels in infrastructure as code, CI/CD automation, and secure cloud architecture, we’d love to hear from you.
Responsibilities
Architect and implement Azure-based infrastructure following Azure Well-Architected Framework principles
Write, review, and maintain Bicep or Terraform IaC modules for infrastructure provisioning
Build and maintain scalable CI/CD pipelines using GitHub Actions or Azure DevOps Pipelines
Manage Azure-native services including VMs, AKS, ACR, App Services, Storage Accounts, SQL Database, Cosmos DB, Azure AD, Front Door, Application Gateway, Azure Monitor/Log Analytics
Design multi-subscription and hub-and-spoke networking topologies using Azure Management Groups, Virtual WAN, Private Link, and RBAC
Deploy, configure, and manage Palo Alto firewalls in Azure, integrating with ExpressRoute, hybrid connectivity, and secure inter-VNet communications
Lead and support application and infrastructure migrations from on-premises into Azure services (including workloads running on platforms such as vSphere and Pure Storage)
Debug and optimize Azure RBAC policies, Managed Identity integrations, and inter-service networking (VNets, NSGs, UDRs, Peering)
Monitor and troubleshoot infrastructure with Azure Monitor, Application Insights, and Dynatrace
Guide security and compliance practices (Azure AD SSO/SAML, Key Vault encryption, Azure Backup/Recovery Vault, resource tagging, policy enforcement, etc.)
Document architecture and processes; support teams across US time zones
Qualifications
Work Authorization: Must be legally authorized to work in the United States without employer sponsorship.
Location Requirement: Must be a resident of the continental United States.

6+ years of hands-on DevOps or Cloud Engineering experience
Deep knowledge of Azure core services, governance, and architecture patterns
Strong experience with Bicep or Terraform
Proficiency in container tooling (Docker, AKS, or App Services)
Solid understanding of Azure AD, RBAC, networking (VNets, NSGs, Palo Alto firewalls, ExpressRoute), and monitoring best practices
Strong scripting skills in Python, Bash, or PowerShell
Understanding of .NET or Java application stacks
Excellent communication skills and experience supporting distributed teams
6+ years of hands-on Azure experience, including production-grade infrastructure and core services
6+ years of experience in a DevOps or Cloud Engineering role, focusing on CI/CD, automation, and infrastructure as code
Nice to Haves
Experience with vSphere and Pure Storage in enterprise environments
Familiarity with hybrid networking scenarios (VPN, ExpressRoute Global Reach)
Exposure to enterprise compliance frameworks (CIS, NIST) and experience collaborating with SOC/SIEM teams
Knowledge of QRadar or similar SIEM platforms
Experience with integration of 3rd party APIs into cloud platforms
Proficiency with Power BI, including building dashboards from Azure service data sources
Exposure to FinOps practices (Azure Cost Management, budgets, and governance for cost optimization)

As a Senior Azure DevOps Engineer, you’ll help architect secure, scalable Azure environments for enterprise clients, partnering with a team that values technical excellence, innovation, and ownership.
We’re seeking engineers who thrive on solving complex challenges, embrace continuous learning, and take pride in delivering quality at scale. If that sounds like you — we’d love to connect.

Please ensure your resume highlights relevant Azure experience, including Terraform or Bicep, CI/CD pipeline management, and Azure networking or security implementations.
