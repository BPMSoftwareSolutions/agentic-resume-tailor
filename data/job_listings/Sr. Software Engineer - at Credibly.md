**Experience Level**
- 6+ years of enterprise technical leadership in software architecture and development
- Focus on service and event-based ecosystems
- **Python experience is required** (though they say "some experience," it's clearly central to the role)

**Technical Skills - Must Have**
- **Python programming** (primary language for the role)
- SQL and NoSQL databases (they use DynamoDB and MySQL)
- Distributed applications and cloud services (AWS-based)

**Leadership & Soft Skills**
- Mentoring and providing technical leadership to junior developers

- Collaboration across engineering, product, data science, and business teams
